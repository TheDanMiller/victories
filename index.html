Mission

To enrich the lives of Veterans, First Responders, and Healthcare workers struggling with depression, PTSD, and other personal issues through caring, compassion, comraderie, and community.

military_tech

Purpose

Our purpose is to enrich the lives of Veterans, First Responders, and Healthcare workers through caring, compassion, camaraderie and community using our unlimited passion for people and sports to promote exciting and entertaining activities that focus on bringing people together and creating a sense of belonging and family.

Outreach

Our goal is to provide assistance for mental health and the wellness of our Veterans, First Responders, and Healthcare workers. We encourage our Veterans, First Responders, Healthcare workers, friends, and family to reach out for assistance, every life matters. Let us help you.

medical_services

How to help

Our Non-Profit is always looking for certified mental health counselors, fellow Veterans, First Responders, and Healthcare workers who are interested in volunteering services to speak with those in need. We are continuing to build and develop our networks of volunteers. Every volunteer and every dollar helps.
